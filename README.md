# 🧠 AI / ML Research Roadmap (12–18 Months)

A structured roadmap to go from **ML foundations → modern generative AI research**.

This guide focuses on **building deep understanding of generative models**, including **GANs, VAEs, Diffusion Models, and modern architectures like DiT**.

---

## 🚀 Roadmap Overview

The roadmap is divided into **5 phases**:

| Phase | Focus | Duration |
|-----|------|------|
| Phase 1 | Foundations | 6–8 weeks |
| Phase 2 | Deep Learning Core | 8–10 weeks |
| Phase 3 | Generative Models | 8–10 weeks |
| Phase 4 | Diffusion Models | 10–12 weeks |
| Phase 5 | Advanced / Research | Ongoing |

---

**Duration:** 6–8 weeks

# 📄 Must-Read Papers (Chronological Order)

1. Auto-Encoding Variational Bayes (2013)
2. Generative Adversarial Networks (2014)
3. Conditional GAN (2014)
4. DCGAN (2015)
5. PixelCNN (2016)
6. Attention Is All You Need (2017)
7. StyleGAN (2018)
8. BERT (2018)
9. Denoising Diffusion Probabilistic Models (2020)
10. CLIP (2021)
11. Latent Diffusion Models (Stable Diffusion) (2022)
12. ControlNet (2023)
13. Diffusion Transformer (DiT) (2023)
